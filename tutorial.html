Tutorial

Welcome to Math Of The Dead. This tutorial will guide you through the main concept and features of this interactive brain game. You will improve your mental math and kill zombies by using mathematical operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division) to get their health to equal zero.



Tutorial

At the top of your screen you will find the pause button, wave number, and your score. Directly beneath this is the bullet queue of 5 numbers which you will use to kill zombies.

Note you may go above and below zero but only a health of exactly zero will kill a zombie.
