Tutorial

Welcome to Math Of The Dead. This tutorial will guide you through the main concept and features of this interactive brain game. You will improve your mental math and kill zombies by using mathematical operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division) to get their health to equal zero.



How To Kill The Zombies

Select a gun and click on a zombie to decrease or increase its health by the number on the bullet icon in the red box at the top of the page.

The currently selected gun is highlighted in grey.

Score is dependant on the highest absolute value you get the zombie to and then kill it. For example if you get the zombies health to (-11) or 11, when you kill it your score will be 11 points for that zombie.



Don't Die!

You must kill zombies before they reach the dotted line at the bottom of the screen or you will lose the game.



Hot Keys

If you are using a computer here are the hot keys on your keyboard:

  • A: Addition Gun
  • S: Subtraction Gun
  • D: Multiplication Gun
  • F: Division Gun
  • E: Skip Current Bullet
