Tutorial

Welcome to Math Of The Dead. This tutorial will guide you through the main concept and features of this interactive brain game. You will improve your mental math and kill zombies by using mathematical operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division) to get their health to equal zero.



Top Bar And Bullet Queue

At the top of your screen you will find the pause button, wave number, and your score. Directly beneath this is the bullet queue of 5 numbers which you will use to kill zombies.

Note you may go above and below zero but only a health of exactly zero will kill a zombie.



Boundary Line and Guns

At the bottom of your screen you will find a dotted horizontal white line. You must kill zombies before they reach this line or you will lose the game.

Beneath this you will find four guns for adding, subtracting, multiplying, and dividing the numbers from the bullet queue against the numbers above the zombies head.



Try it out!

To get a feel for the game try pressing all boxes with a red background.

Note the addition gun has a grey background at the moment because that is the currently selected gun.

Your first wave will not begin until all buttons have been pressed once.



Bullet Queue

These are the numbers you will use to kill the zombies.

The one you are currently using is in the red box, you can use this by clicking on a zombie, or skip it by clicking on the red box.



Addition Gun

Each time you use this gun you will be performing addition with the numbers from the bullet queue and the numbers above the zombies head.

For example if the current bullet is ( -1) and you click on a zombie with (-1) health you will cause the zombie’s health to equal -2.



Subtraction Gun

Each time you use this gun you will be performing subtraction with the numbers from the bullet queue and the numbers above the zombies head.

For example if the current bullet is ( -1) and you click on a zombie with (-1) health you will cause the zombie’s health to equal 0.



Multiplication Gun

Each time you use this gun you will be performing multiplication with the numbers from the bullet queue and the numbers above the zombies head.

For example if the current bullet is ( -1) and you click on a zombie with (-1) health you will cause the zombie’s health to equal 1.



Division Gun

Each time you use this gun you will be performing division with the numbers from the bullet queue and the numbers above the zombies head.

For example if the current bullet is ( -1) and you click on a zombie with (-1) health you will cause the zombie’s health to equal 1. Also this gun rounds up if you attempt to divide 15 by 7 you will cause the zombie’s health to be 3.
